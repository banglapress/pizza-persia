Neapolitan Pizza

Neapolitan is the original pizza. This delicious pie dates all the way back to 18th century in Naples, Italy. Pizza Marinara features tomatoes, garlic, oregano, and extra virgin olive oil. Pizza Margherita features tomatoes, sliced mozzarella, basil, and extra virgin olive oil.

Greek Pizza

Greek pizza was created by Greek immigrants who came to America and were introduced to Italian pizza. Traditional Greek Pizza topped with cheese, which is usually a mix of mozzarella and cheddar or provolone. It may feature a variety of non-Greek or Greek toppings, such as feta cheese, black olives, and red onion.

Persian Pizza

Persian pizza is characterized by its thick bread and large amounts of cheese. Traditional Persian pizza is served in Iran and places affected by the Iranian diaspora. It is usually made with minced meat, beef sausage, bell pepper, mushroom, mozzarella cheese, and Persian spices.
